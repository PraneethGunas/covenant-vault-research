## TL;DR

**Primary target:** Financial Cryptography 2027 main conference
- Deadline: ~Sep 15, 2026
- Format: 15pp Springer LNCS, double-blind
- Acceptance: 22–25%
- Why: FC'25 accepted directly comparable formal-verification-of-Bitcoin work; community understands BIPs without exposition

**Parallel action:** Post to IACR ePrint + arXiv by ~May 20, 2026 (2–3 weeks post-defense)

**Backup cascade:**
1. CAAW @ FC 2027 (workshop, ~Jan 2027)
2. ICBC 2028 SoK (~Dec 2027)
3. AFT 2027 (~May 2027)
4. MARBLE 2027 (~Feb 2027)

**Avoid:** USENIX Security, IEEE S&P, ACM CCS main, Scaling Bitcoin (dormant), CESC (dormant), SBC/DSS/RWC (talk-only)

## First action after thesis defense

1. May 1–7, 2026: Submit final thesis to VT
2. May 8–14: Port `thesis.tex` → `llncs` LaTeX skeleton
3. May 15: Upload preprint to IACR ePrint (non-anonymized, full 100pp version)
4. May 20: Mirror on arXiv (cs.CR primary)
5. May 22: Announce on bitcoin-dev mailing list + Delving Bitcoin
6. Jun–Aug: Iteratively prepare 15pp anonymized FC'27 version
7. Sep 15: Submit to FC'27

See `TIMELINE.md` for full schedule.
